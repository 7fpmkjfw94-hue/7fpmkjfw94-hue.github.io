Capturing Wild Moments

I started my photography journey during highschool when I had a kickass photography teacher named Mr. Oleson for a visual arts class. I realized quickly my love for photography and bought a Canon EOS 250D. Ended up having the opportunity to go to Alaska on a National Geographic Linblad boat and met a bunch of photographers-- but the most important one was Cindy Mirabella. She gave me invaluable advice and insight into the world of photography and at the end of our trip she offered to give me some of her old crop sensor sony lenses. My newly acquired nifty-50 and 36-210mm lenses became my bread and butter as soon as I upgraded to a Sony a6400. Since then I've been taking my camera around everytime I go on a trip. Ive found that photography really helps me appreciate the outdoors and wildlife to a further degree. Hope you enjoy my portfolio.
